Welcome to the comprehensive guide for managing companies on LVLX. This documentation covers everything you need to know about company registration, management, and administration.

## Company Registration Process

### Step 1: Initial Registration

1. Navigate to the Companies section
2. Click "Add New" button
3. Complete the registration form:
   - Company Name
   - Department
   - Physical Address
   - Registration Number (Format: YYYY/NNNNN/LL)
   - Employee Count
   - Company Type (Private, Public, or Organisation)
   - Company Description

### Step 2: Document Verification

1. Upload Registration Certificate
   - Must be in PDF format
   - File size limit: 10MB
   - Clear and legible scan
2. Wait for verification (24-48 hours)
3. Track verification status in dashboard

- New company registration: 100 points
