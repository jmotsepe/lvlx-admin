## Overview

The LVLX points system enables access to premium features and services on the platform. Points can be purchased and used for various activities such as posting vacancies, accessing candidate information, and scheduling interviews.

## Using Points

1. Vacancy Features:
   ```
   - Post new vacancy: 5 points
   - Boost visibility: 10 points
   - Extended duration: 15 points
   - Featured listing: 25 points
   ```

2. Candidate Access:
   ```
   - View detailed profile: 1 point
   - Contact candidate: 2 points
   - Schedule interview: 5 points
   - Access premium filters: 10 points
   ```
