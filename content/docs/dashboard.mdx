Welcome to your LVLX Dashboard! This comprehensive guide will help you understand and make the most of all dashboard features available to you. The dashboard is your central hub for managing all aspects of your LVLX experience.

## Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is personalized based on your user type (Job Seeker, Employer, or Administrator). Here's what you'll find in each version:

### Job Seeker Dashboard

#### 1. Profile Management

- **Profile Completion Meter**: Track your profile completeness
- **Quick Edit Options**: Update key information
- **Profile Visibility Settings**: Control who sees your information
- **Professional Summary**: Showcase your expertise

#### 2. Resume Center

- **Resume Upload**: Add new resumes (PDF format)
- **Resume Manager**: Organize multiple versions
- **Application Tracking**: See which resume was used where
- **Document Verification**: Status of verified documents

#### 3. Job Applications

- **Active Applications**: Currently under consideration
- **Application History**: Past applications
- **Status Tracking**: Real-time application status
- **Interview Schedule**: Upcoming interviews

#### 4. Saved Items

- **Saved Jobs**: Bookmarked positions
- **Followed Companies**: Companies you're interested in
- **Saved Searches**: Quick access to frequent searches
- **Notes**: Personal notes on applications

### Employer Dashboard

#### 1. Company Profile

- **Company Information**: Manage company details
- **Verification Status**: Track document verification
- **Team Management**: Add/remove team members
- **Company Settings**: Update preferences

#### 2. Vacancy Management

- **Active Listings**: Currently open positions
- **Draft Listings**: Save works in progress
- **Expired Listings**: Archive of past positions
- **Quick Post**: Template-based job posting

#### 3. Candidate Management

- **Application Review**: Screen incoming applications
- **Candidate Pipeline**: Track candidate progress
- **Interview Calendar**: Schedule and manage interviews
- **Communication Center**: Message candidates

#### 4. Points & Analytics

- **Points Balance**: Track available points
- **Usage History**: Point transaction history
- **Performance Metrics**: Listing performance
- **Engagement Analytics**: Candidate interaction data

### Administrator Dashboard

#### 1. Platform Overview

- **User Statistics**: Active users and registration trends
- **Company Verifications**: Pending and completed
- **System Health**: Platform performance metrics
- **Support Tickets**: Outstanding issues

#### 2. User Management

- **User Directory**: Search and manage users
- **Role Assignment**: Update user permissions
- **Account Status**: Active/Inactive accounts
- **Verification Queue**: Document verification tasks

#### 3. Content Management

- **Job Listings**: Monitor and moderate posts
- **Company Profiles**: Review and verify
- **System Announcements**: Create and manage
- **Help Center**: Update support content
