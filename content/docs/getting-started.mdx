Welcome to your journey with LVLX! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with our platform. Whether you're a job seeker, employer, or administrator, we've got you covered.

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit the LVLX homepage
2. Click the "Get Started" button
3. Choose your account type:
   - Job Seeker
   - Employer
   - Sponsor
4. Fill in your basic information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (must be secure)
   - Contact number
5. Verify your email address through the link we send you

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

#### For Job Seekers:

1. Add your professional information:
   - Profile picture
   - Contact details
   - Professional summary
   - Work experience
   - Education history
2. Upload your resume:
   - PDF format preferred
   - Include relevant certificates
   - Add a cover letter (optional)

#### For Employers:

1. Add your company details:
   - Company name
   - Business registration number
   - Company address
   - Department information
2. Upload required documents:
   - Company registration certificate (PDF)
   - Other relevant documentation
3. Wait for verification (usually 24-48 hours)

## Navigating the Dashboard

### Job Seekers Dashboard

- **Profile Section**: Update your professional information
- **Applications**: Track your job applications
- **Resume Manager**: Upload and manage multiple resumes
- **Saved Jobs**: Access your bookmarked opportunities
- **Messages**: Communicate with potential employers

### Employer Dashboard

- **Company Profile**: Manage your company information
- **Vacancies**: Post and manage job listings
- **Applications**: Review candidate applications
- **Team Management**: Add and manage team members
- **Points Balance**: Track and use your platform points

## Using Key Features

### For Job Seekers

#### Searching for Jobs

1. Use the search bar to find relevant positions
2. Filter by:
   - Location
   - Industry
   - Job type
   - Salary range
3. Save interesting positions for later

#### Applying for Jobs

1. Select a position
2. Choose your resume
3. Add a cover letter (if required)
4. Submit your application
5. Track application status

### For Employers

#### Posting a Vacancy

1. Click "Post New Vacancy"
2. Fill in job details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Requirements
   - Salary range
   - Location
3. Set application deadline
4. Use points to boost visibility

#### Managing Applications

1. Review incoming applications
2. Sort and filter candidates
3. Schedule interviews
4. Track candidate progress
5. Communicate with applicants

## Points System

### Earning Points

- Complete profile: 50 points
- Post vacancies: 10 points per post
- Receive applications: 5 points per application
- Successful hires: 100 points

### Using Points

- Boost job postings
- Access premium features
- Extend posting duration
- Highlight company profile
