## Overview

The interview process in LVLX is designed to facilitate seamless communication between employers and candidates. This guide explains how interviews are scheduled, conducted, and managed on the platform.

## For Candidates

### Interview Notifications

1. When selected for an interview:
   ```
   - You'll receive an email notification
   - Your application status changes to "Interview"
   - Interview details appear in your dashboard
   ```

### Types of Interviews

LVLX supports two interview formats:

1. Physical Interviews:
   ```
   - In-person meetings
   - Specific location provided
   - Date and time specified
   - Additional instructions if any
   ```

2. Online Interviews:
   ```
   - Virtual meetings
   - Meeting link provided
   - Platform details (e.g., Zoom, Teams)
   - Technical requirements
   ```

### Preparing for Your Interview

1. Physical Interview Preparation:
   ```
   - Confirm location details
   - Plan your route
   - Arrive 15 minutes early
   - Bring required documents
   ```

2. Online Interview Preparation:
   ```
   - Test your internet connection
   - Check camera and microphone
   - Install required software
   - Join 5 minutes early
   ```

### Interview Day Guidelines

1. General Tips:
   ```
   - Review the vacancy details
   - Prepare relevant examples
   - Dress professionally
   - Bring your resume
   ```

2. Online Etiquette:
   ```
   - Find a quiet space
   - Ensure good lighting
   - Maintain eye contact
   - Minimize background noise
   ```

## For Employers

### Scheduling Interviews

1. From Application Management:
   ```
   - Select candidate
   - Choose "Schedule Interview"
   - Set date and time
   - Select interview type
   ```

2. Required Details:
   ```
   - Interview date
   - Time slot
   - Type (Online/Physical)
   - Location or meeting link
   - Description/instructions
   ```

### Interview Management

1. Track Interviews:
   ```
   - View upcoming interviews
   - Monitor candidate responses
   - Manage schedule changes
   - Record outcomes
   ```

2. Best Practices:
   ```
   - Send clear instructions
   - Provide contact information
   - Allow buffer time
   - Document feedback
   ```

### Points Usage

Interview scheduling costs:
```
- Scheduling an interview: 5 points
- Rescheduling: No additional cost
- Cancellation: Points not refunded
```
