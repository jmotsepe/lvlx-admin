Welcome to LVLX, your comprehensive platform for connecting businesses with talent and managing professional growth opportunities. This guide will help you understand how to make the most of our platform, whether you're a job seeker, employer, or administrator.

## What is LVLX?

LVLX is a modern platform designed to bridge the gap between companies and talented individuals. Our platform offers:

- **For Job Seekers**: Create professional profiles, upload resumes, and apply for opportunities
- **For Companies**: Post vacancies, manage applications, and find the perfect candidates
- **For Administrators**: Oversee platform operations and ensure quality control

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Profile Management

- Create and maintain your professional profile
- Upload and manage multiple resumes
- Track your application history

### 2. Company Dashboard

- Register and verify your company
- Post and manage job vacancies
- Track applicant progress

### 3. Points System

- Earn points through platform engagement
- Use points for premium features
- Track your point balance and history

## Getting Started

1. **Create Your Account**: Sign up and verify your email
2. **Complete Your Profile**: Add your professional information
3. **Explore Opportunities**: Browse vacancies or post new ones
4. **Engage with the Platform**: Apply, manage, and grow
