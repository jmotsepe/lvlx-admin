## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Account

1. Go to www.lvlx.com/signup
2. Click "Register as Employer"
3. Fill in your personal details:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password
   - Contact number
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email through the link sent to your inbox

### 2. Setting Up Your Company Profile

1. After email verification, log in to your account
2. Click "Complete Company Profile" on your dashboard
3. Fill in required company information:
   ```
   Company Name: Your registered business name
   Registration Number: Format YYYY/NNNNN/LL
   Physical Address: Your business address
   Department: Main department (e.g., Human Resources)
   Employee Count: Current number of employees
   Company Type: Select Private, Public, or Organisation
   ```
4. Write a detailed company description
5. Upload your company registration certificate (PDF format)
6. Click "Submit for Verification"

### 3. While Waiting for Verification

- You can browse the platform
- Set up your team structure
- Prepare job descriptions
- Note: You cannot post vacancies until verified

## Posting Your First Job

### 1. Creating a Vacancy

1. From your dashboard, click "Post New Vacancy"
2. Fill in the job details:
   ```
   Title: Position name
   Description: Detailed job description
   Location: Work location
   Salary Range: Monthly salary (optional)
   Closing Date: Application deadline
   Job Type: Contract/Permanent/Temporary
   Remote Work: Yes/No
   ```
3. Click "Preview" to review
4. Click "Post Vacancy" (uses 10 points)

### 2. Managing Your Posting

1. Go to "Active Vacancies"
2. For each vacancy, you can:
   - Click "Edit" to update details
   - Click "Boost" to increase visibility (uses points)
   - Click "Close" to end applications early
   - Click "Applications" to view candidates

## Handling Applications

### 1. Reviewing Candidates

1. Go to "Applications" under your vacancy
2. For each application:
   - Click candidate name to view profile
   - Click "Download Resume" for their CV
   - Use status buttons to update their status:
     ```
     [Pending] → [Shortlist] or [Reject]
     [Shortlisted] → [Schedule Interview] or [Reject]
     [Interview] → [Hire] or [Reject]
     ```

### 2. Scheduling Interviews

1. Click "Schedule Interview" for shortlisted candidates
2. Fill in interview details:
   ```
   Date: Select interview date
   Time: Choose time slot
   Type: Physical or Online
   Location/Link: Add meeting place or video call link
   Notes: Any special instructions
   ```
3. Click "Send Invitation"
4. Track responses in "Interview Calendar"

## Managing Your Team

### 1. Adding Team Members

1. Go to "Team Management"
2. Click "Add Manager"
3. Enter their details:
   ```
   Email: Work email address
   Role: Select role type
   Department: Assign department
   ```
4. Click "Send Invitation"
5. They'll receive an email to join

### 2. Setting Permissions

1. Go to "Team Settings"
2. Click on team member
3. Select permissions:
   - Vacancy Management
   - Application Review
   - Interview Scheduling
   - Points Management

## Points System

### 1. Checking Your Balance

1. Click "Points" in top navigation
2. View:
   - Current balance
   - Usage history
   - Purchase options

### 2. Buying Points

1. Go to "Buy Points"
2. Select package:
   ```
   Basic: 100 points - $X
   Standard: 500 points - $Y
   Premium: 1000 points - $Z
   ```
3. Click "Purchase"
4. Complete payment

### 3. Points Usage Guide

- Posting vacancy: 10 points
- Boosting vacancy: 5 points/day
- Accessing candidate search: 1 point/search
- Scheduling interview: 5 points
- Sending candidate invite: 1 point

## Communication Tools

### 1. Messaging Candidates

1. Open candidate's application
2. Click "Message"
3. Type your message
4. Click "Send"
5. Track conversations in "Messages"

### 2. Team Chat

1. Go to "Team Chat"
2. Select team member or create group
3. Use chat features:
   - Text messages
   - File sharing
   - Interview notes
   - Task assignments

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### 1. Can't Post Vacancy?

- Check verification status
- Ensure sufficient points
- Verify all required fields
- Check file size limits

### 2. Application Issues?

1. Clear browser cache
2. Log out and back in
3. Contact support if persists:
   ```
   Email: support@lvlx.com
   Phone: XXX-XXX-XXXX
   Live Chat: Click bottom right icon
   ```
