## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Account

1. Go to www.lvlx.com/signup
2. Click "Register as Sponsor"
3. Fill in your details:
   ```
   First Name: Your first name
   Last Name: Your last name
   Email: Your email address
   Password: Create a strong password
   Cell Number: Your contact number
   Province: Your location
   ```
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email

### 2. Setting Up Your Sponsor Profile

1. After verification, complete your profile:
   - Upload organization logo
   - Add organization details
   - Specify sponsorship interests
   - Set sponsorship budget

## Sponsorship Management

### 1. Viewing Youth Profiles

1. Go to "Youth Directory"
2. Filter candidates by:
   ```
   Location: Province/City
   Education: Level/Field
   Skills: Required abilities
   Experience: Years/Type
   ```
3. Click on profiles to view details

### 2. Selecting Candidates

1. Review candidate profiles:
   - Education history
   - Work experience
   - Skills and qualifications
   - Career objectives
2. Add promising candidates to your shortlist
3. Click "Contact" to initiate communication

### 3. Sponsorship Process

1. Select candidate from shortlist
2. Click "Initiate Sponsorship"
3. Choose sponsorship type:
   ```
   Full Sponsorship: All costs covered
   Partial Sponsorship: Specific aspects
   Mentorship: Professional guidance
   Training: Skill development
   ```
4. Set sponsorship terms:
   - Duration
   - Coverage details
   - Requirements
   - Milestones
