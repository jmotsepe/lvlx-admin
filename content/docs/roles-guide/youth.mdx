## Getting Started

### 1. Creating Your Account

1. Go to www.lvlx.com/signup
2. Click "Register as Job Seeker"
3. Fill in your personal details:
   ```
   First Name: Your first name
   Last Name: Your last name
   Email: Your email address
   Password: Create a strong password
   Cell Number: Your contact number
   Province: Your location
   Gender: Select from options
   ```
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email through the link sent to your inbox

### 2. Setting Up Your Profile

1. After email verification, log in to your account
2. Click "Complete Profile" on your dashboard
3. Fill in your personal information:
   - Upload a professional photo
   - Add your contact details
   - Select your province
   - Write a professional summary

### 3. Creating Your First Resume

You have two options for adding your resume:

#### Option A: Upload Existing Resume

1. Go to "Resume Manager"
2. Click "Upload Resume"
3. Select your PDF resume file
4. Add a name for this resume version
5. Click "Upload"

#### Option B: Build Custom Resume

1. Go to "Resume Builder"
2. Click "Create New Resume"
3. Fill in each section:

   **Personal Details:**

   ```
   Full Name: As per ID
   Gender: Select option
   Home Language: Your primary language
   Country: Your location
   Address: Current address
   ```

   **Education Details:**

   ```
   Title: Qualification name
   Institution: School/University name
   Status: Completed/Active/Not Completed
   Year Started: Start year
   Year Completed: End year (if applicable)
   ```

   **Work Experience:**

   ```
   Company: Previous employer
   Position: Job title
   Dates: Employment period
   Current Job: Yes/No
   Reason for Leaving: If applicable
   ```

   **Contact Information:**

   ```
   Email: Professional email
   Cell Number: Active number
   Tel Number: Optional
   Next of Kin: Emergency contact
   ```

   **Career Objectives:**

   - Write your career goals
   - Highlight key aspirations
   - Mention target roles

   **References:**

   ```
   Name: Reference person
   Contact: Their number/email
   Institution: Company/Organization
   ```

4. Click "Preview" to review
5. Click "Save Resume"

## Finding and Applying for Jobs

### 1. Searching for Jobs

1. Go to "Job Search" on your dashboard
2. Use search filters:
   ```
   Keywords: Job title or skills
   Location: Preferred area
   Job Type: Contract/Permanent/Temporary
   Remote Work: Yes/No
   Salary Range: Expected range
   ```
3. Click "Search"
4. Save searches you want to monitor

### 2. Applying for Jobs

1. Open an interesting job posting
2. Click "Apply Now"
3. Select which resume to use
4. Add/edit cover letter
5. Review all details
6. Click "Submit Application"
7. Track in "My Applications"

### 3. Managing Applications

1. Go to "My Applications"
2. View status of each application:
   ```
   Pending: Under review
   Shortlisted: Selected for next stage
   Interview: Selected for interview
   Rejected: Not selected
   Hired: Job offered
   ```
3. Click on any application to:
   - View job details
   - Check application status
   - Message employer
   - Schedule interview (if invited)

## Interview Management

### 1. Preparing for Interviews

1. Check "Interview Invitations" regularly
2. For each invitation:
   - Confirm date and time
   - Note location/online link
   - Review job details
   - Prepare required documents

### 2. Online Interviews

1. Test your equipment beforehand:
   - Camera working
   - Microphone clear
   - Internet stable
2. Join link 5 minutes early
3. Have your resume ready
4. Find a quiet location

### 3. Physical Interviews

1. Save location details
2. Plan your route
3. Arrive 15 minutes early
4. Bring printed resume
5. Dress professionally

## Communication Center

### 1. Messaging Employers

1. Open the application
2. Click "Message"
3. Write your message
4. Click "Send"
5. Check "Messages" daily

### 2. Notification Settings

1. Go to "Settings"
2. Under "Notifications":
   - Set email preferences
   - Set SMS alerts
   - Choose notification types

## Profile Management

### 1. Updating Your Profile

1. Click "Edit Profile"
2. Update any section:
   - Personal details
   - Contact information
   - Professional summary
   - Resume versions

### 2. Privacy Settings

1. Go to "Privacy Settings"
2. Choose who can see:
   - Your profile
   - Contact details
   - Application history
